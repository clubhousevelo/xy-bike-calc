▼ Privacy Policy

Information about how we handle and protect your data.

This Privacy Policy describes how XY Bike Calculator ("we", "our", or "the application") handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is managed.

1. Information Collection and Storage

Our application stores all data locally on your device using browser storage mechanisms. We collect and store:

  • Calculator settings and measurements
  • Client names (when provided)
  • Bike configurations and measurements
  • User preferences (such as dark/light mode settings)
  • Search parameters and results

2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored is used solely for:

  • Saving your calculator configurations and measurements
  • Maintaining your preferences between sessions
  • Providing a convenient way to access your previous calculations
  • Improving your user experience by remembering your settings

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • localStorage: For persistent data storage across browser sessions
  • sessionStorage: For temporary data storage during your browser session

No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers, except when accessing our bike database through the Google Sheets API.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Google Sheets API: Used exclusively for retrieving bike database information. No personal data is sent to Google's servers.

5. Your Data Control Rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Clear all stored data using your browser's settings
  • Use the application's reset/clear functions to remove specific data
  • Choose not to save certain information by not using the save features

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Our application uses localStorage and sessionStorage to function properly. These storage mechanisms are necessary for:

  • Saving your calculator configurations
  • Maintaining your preferences
  • Providing a seamless user experience

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or support channels.
